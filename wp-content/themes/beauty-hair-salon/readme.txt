== Description ==

Beauty Hair Salon brings a suitable design that works wonderfully with hair salons, beauty salons, hair spas, hairdressers, beauty shops, cosmetics, health and lifestyle, massage, salon, wellness and spa, homemade soaps, organic beauty products shops, nail salons, and more. Designed with a clean interface and secure codes, this theme has the expertise of experienced WP developers and brings you minimal design that focuses on the content itself. This elegant theme is made retina-ready to bring a design that is friendly with all the screen sizes and gives a fantastic experience to the users. Call to Action Buttons (CTA) are also included acting as a guide to users. SEO-friendly codes of this theme are well-optimized to give you a faster page load time.
